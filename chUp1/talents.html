What is Hololive?
Hololive Production (ホロライブプロダクション Hororaibu Purodakushon), or simply known as hololive (ホロライブ), is an agency of Virtual YouTubers created by Cover Corp.


What is a Virtual Youtuber?
A virtual YouTuber (バーチャルユーチューバー, bācharu yūchūbā) or VTuber (ブイチューバー, buichūbā) is an online entertainer, typically a Japanese or English speaking YouTuber and/or live streamer, who is represented by a (usually anime-inspired) digital avatar generated by computer graphics such as Live2D.
Most vtubers also have their own lore and story background that is tied to their character.
